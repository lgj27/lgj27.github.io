Bio

I am a natvie Austinite, born and raised. I have been a Hair Stylist for 39 years and continue to enjoy and love what I do. I enjoy all my clients' company and their business and pride myself in fulfilling all their needs and expectations. I continue to attend hair shows regularly to stay up to date with new styles, trends and different ways of doing hair. Satisfaction is my number one priority. I've been married for 39 years to my wife, Mary Helen, and we have 2 sons that we've put through college. My eldest son, Luis, is a Computer Science Graduate and my youngest son, JT, is an Electrical Engineer Graduate. My hobbies are, running, weight lifting, working on my yard, enjoying the outdoors, getting together with family, watching football and baseball. I enjoy cookouts on my free time, working on the house and maintaining my vehicles. I enjoy eating out with family and I enjoy going to church.
