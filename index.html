About Me

Programmer/Software & Web Dev.

I love technology and learning how to develop, create, tinker and manipulate the way a program/software/website looks and operates. When I got my first Samsung Galaxy smartphone and got introduced to developing and learning how to modify the UI and customize different areas of my phone, on a developing website, I became so fascinated with what I did to change the UI and have a different experience with my phone. Now, although I didn't design the UI and how it functions, I was very intrigued in learning how it was done and how you go about learning these types of things. This led me to pursuing Computer Science in college. After learning all I could in school, it pushed me towards wanting to learn and know more. Since graduation and while searching for a job in my field of study, I continue to learn, read, and study new forms of languages and where I can take this knowledge. I know there's always new ways to go about writing code and you never know enough. I'm proficient and confident in C++, although I know there's so much more to learn and understand. I've taken the understanding of C++ and taught myself other languages such as Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS so I can get more experience and broaden my understanding of how these languages operate. Moving forward, I continue to learn and understand what I can do with each language and the possibilities.

Web Development Services

This field kind of took me by surprise. While working on my own website, I had never been exposed to the language or everything necessary to put a website together and get it published to the internet, but I was interested and intrigued. As I took every measure necessary to learn every bit of detail in the structure, language and syntax, I lost myself in the development process. I’m naturally very meticulous in everything that I do in life, and this was no different. Once I completed my website and had it up and going, I realized something. Not only did I teach myself everything necessary, but I enjoyed every bit of it. This was a similar process I enjoyed almost parallel to customizing my phone at a younger age. It’s with this knowledge and process that I now take into building any website that would require my services.
